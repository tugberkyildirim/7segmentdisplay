• a circuit made using a 74hc595 integrated circuit to reduce the 7-segment display from 8 pins to 4 pins.

74HC595 PINS FOR ARDUINO
 • SH_CP
 • ST_CP
 • DS
 • Maximum 5 volts
 • GND

   Sketch > Include Library > Add .ZIP library
